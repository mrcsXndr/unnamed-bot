# Feedback: prefer concise, action-first replies

> Example feedback note. Delete or replace with your own as the bot learns how
> you like to work. Notes in `memory/feedback/` capture preferences and
> corrections so the bot doesn't repeat the same misses.

- Lead with the action or answer; skip preamble and filler.
- Bullets and numbers over prose paragraphs when listing.
- Don't restate the question back before answering.
- When replying on a mobile channel (e.g. Telegram), keep it short.
